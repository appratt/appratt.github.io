Home

Hi! Andrew Plemmons Pratt here. This site is a little workshop where tinker with learning and making. Professionally, I weave together innovation and race equity work at CityBridge Education. Elsewhere, I bake bread, play the piano, play boardgames with my two kids, and support work on racial justice and inclusion in DC public schools.

In May 2022, I'm setting out on a sabbatical from work. I have a long list of projects I want to work on, including revamping this site.
